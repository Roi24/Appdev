Requirements


1. BIRTH CERTIFICATE of child, from municipality

2. For the Parents or Guardians, MARRAGE CONTRACT (if maried in catholic church) or BAPTISMAL CONFIORMATION CERTIFICATE.

3. For the God Father and God Mother, MARRACE CONTRACT or BAPTISMAL CONFIRMATION CERTIFICATE.

4. SEMINAR CARD, proof of Parents or Guardian and God Parents.

5. PERMIT (ORIGINAL COPY), from the Parish of IMMACULATE CONCEPTION.

* for the child above 7 years old, communicate to Parish Office for the other Requirements.
